HUNTER INVESTMENTS LLC

Hunter Investments and The Renaissance Companies joined forces to create Hunter Renaissance Development LLC and www.HRD.zone to focus on growing neighborhoods, based in Central Oregon with offices in Chicago, IL.

Hunter Investments LLC is focused on the unique challenges within the real estate development environment. With principal’s experience in most aspects of development disciplines, Hunter Investments delivers solutions to a challenging marketplace. By mitigating market demands with environmental and regulatory policies, equities in the development decisions are created to provide beneficial results.

Neil Amondson, principal and founder of Hunter Investments has been involved in most aspects of the real estate development spectrum. Amondson, a former WA State Representative and Senator for SW Washington (1987-’94) has a “hands-on” understanding of the land use arena from both a regulatory and developer perspective. With experience in civil construction throughout the western United States, Amondson served as manager for Sovran Development Group in providing one of the largest Urban Growth Area (UGA) expansions in WA state history.

Key to Amondson’s success, is the ability to bring diverse stakeholder opinions and objectives into a consensus based direction. Dedicated to positive outcomes, Hunter Investments pursues projects that bring value and creative opportunities to investors.

Hunter Investments experience spans from project management to governmental affairs for a diverse portfolio of projects including; marine, commercial and large scale industrial projects throughout the US. Projects included Liberty Plaza in Chehalis, WA, various marine and commercial projects throughout the Pacific NW and his Career Bakken Achievement in the Bakken oil fields.
